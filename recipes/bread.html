French Bread

Description

A timeless classic, French bread makes for a great snack on its own or as an addition to nearly any meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons yeast
  • 1/2 cup warm water
  • 2 cups hot water
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1/3 cup oil
  • 6 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 egg, beaten

Cooking Instructions

  1. Dissolve yeast into 1/2 cup warm water in a small bowl. Let proof for 10 minutes.
  2. In a seperate mixing bowl, combine hot water, sugar, salt, oil, and 3 cups of flour. Mix together then add yeast to mixture.
  3. Add remaining 3 1/2 cups of flour, 1 cup at a time. Continue mixing with each addition.
  4. When dough starts to pull away from bowl, set it aside to sit for 10 minutes.
  5. Seperate dough into 3 pieces on a floured surface. Roll each piece into a 9 x 12 inch rectangle.
  6. Roll each piece up like a jelly roll and shape into a loaf. Smooth out the edges.
  7. Place dough on a greased baking pan, seam side down. Use a knife to make 3-4 diagonal cuts about 1/4 inch thick in dough.
  8. Brush dough with beaten egg. Let dough rise for 30-40 minutes in a warm place.
  9. Bake at 375 degrees for 18-22 minutes or until golden brown.
  10. Allow bread to cool then slice into desired size pieces. Enjoy!
